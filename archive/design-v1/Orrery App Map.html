◇ DESIGN ROADMAP · APR 2026

Every screen in Observatory QA, mapped to the orrery system.

The orrery aesthetic spans the whole product, but the metaphor's intensity varies with what each screen is for. Below: every page in the codebase, sorted by surface area and tagged by intensity tier and design status. This is the working map for what gets designed next.

FULL
Full orrery
Star + orbits + planets are the hero. Editorial voice on summaries. Where the metaphor does real work.
QUIET
Quiet orrery
Mostly utilitarian layout with a small celestial element — mini-map, arc, sparkline-as-orbit. Voice in Ory's Note only.
BENCH
Workbench
No celestial element. Same type, color, owl mark, ◇ tags. Reads as same product, looks like a calm work surface.

◇ NEXT UP · IF YOU AGREE
Six items marked NEXT UP form the next sprint:
  1. Coaching — full-orrery hero, the largest missing feature.
  2. Coaching session — quiet companion, proves the pair holds together.
  3. Insights — first real owl-as-co-worker surface.
  4. Clinical notes editor — workbench stress test. If it holds here, it holds everywhere.
  5. Settings shell + one tab — bench tier on the most mundane surface possible.
  6. Owl persona kit + Realism states + Mobile — three cross-cutting passes that lift everything we already have.
My pick to start: Clinical notes editor. If the workbench tier doesn't hold on a 41k-line SOAP/DAP/BIRP editor, the whole "intensity-tiered system" idea is in trouble — better to find that out first. Coaching is a close second if you'd rather lead with the visible new feature.
